THE PURPOSE

In integration and advanced math studies, students will come across problems with fractions containing polynomials in the denominator.  They can be solved more easily if the fractions are simplified through partial fraction decomposition, which is a process that works backwards based on the concept of adding fractions by obtaining a common denominator.
